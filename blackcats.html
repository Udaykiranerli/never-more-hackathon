Blackcats

mysterious and elusive guardians

Explore hidden patterns in crop health and protective mechanisms for sustainable farming. Blackcats embody the vigilant protectors that safeguard harvests in the shadows of the fields.

Domain: Smart Agriculture

Problem Statements:

  1. CEC-PS-ID-01 - Crop Advisory Web Application:
    Farmers need simple guidance on suitable crops, fertilizers, and seasons based on basic inputs like location and soil type.
  2. CEC-PS-ID-02 - Leaf Disease Image Classifier:
    Early identification of crop diseases can reduce losses. Develop a basic image-based disease detection system.
  3. CEC-PS-ID-03 - Smart Irrigation Decision Support System:
    Farmers overuse water due to lack of irrigation timing insights. Create a dashboard that suggests irrigation based on moisture and weather data.
  4. CEC-PS-ID-04 - Farmer Market Price Tracker:
    Farmers lack awareness of current market prices. Build a simple platform showing daily crop prices.
  5. CEC-PS-ID-05 - Fertilizer Recommendation Tool:
    Improper fertilizer usage reduces yield. Develop a rule-based recommendation system.
  6. CEC-PS-ID-06 - Weather Alert System for Farmers:
    Unexpected weather damages crops. Create an alert system for rainfall and temperature extremes.
