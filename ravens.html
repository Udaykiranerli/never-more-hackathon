NeverMore Ravens

healing and compassionate caretakers

Supportive solutions for community health and well-being. Ravens represent guardians that connect people to care, timely information, and helpful resources.

Domain: Healthcare & Well-being

Problem Statements:

  1. CEC-PS-ID-07 - Symptom-Based Health Checker:
    Users need preliminary health advice before visiting hospitals. Develop a symptom input and guidance system.
  2. CEC-PS-ID-08 - Medicine Reminder Application:
    Patients often forget medicines. Build a reminder app with alerts.
  3. CEC-PS-ID-09 - Health Report Storage System:
    Patients struggle to manage medical reports. Create a digital health record manager.
  4. CEC-PS-ID-10 - Basic Fitness Tracking Dashboard:
    Users want to monitor daily activity. Develop a dashboard using manual or simulated data.
  5. CEC-PS-ID-11 - Mental Wellness Support Chatbot:
    Students lack accessible mental wellness support. Build a simple chatbot for guidance and resources.
  6. CEC-PS-ID-12 - Appointment Booking System for Clinics:
    Manual appointment systems cause delays. Create an online booking system.
