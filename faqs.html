FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

What lurks in the shadows of Never More?
The Never More Hackathon is a 2-day coding extravaganza where teams of nocturnal innovators battle to create groundbreaking solutions under the veil of eternal world.
How do I join the coven of coders?
Venture forth to our registration portal and submit your details. Choose to join an existing team or forge your own alliance of outcasts.
What dark arts and arcane tools are permitted?
All major programming languages, frameworks, and mystical APIs are allowed, as long as they don't summon forbidden entities or violate the sacred rules.
Will there be treasures for the victorious?
Yes, the triumphant teams shall receive coveted trophies, certificates of arcane mastery, and substantial cash prizes to fuel their future endeavors.
What if I become ensnared in the labyrinth of code?
Fear not, for our organizers—the keepers of ancient knowledge—will be present throughout the event to guide lost souls and unravel tangled spells.
Is sustenance provided for these nocturnal creatures?
Indeed, meals, snacks, and elixirs of energy will be served at regular intervals to maintain your vitality through the long night.
Can I bring my faithful familiar?
While pets are not permitted in the sanctum, your enchanted laptop shall be your most trusted companion in this digital ritual.
What happens if the veil of power fails?
We have summoned backup generators to ensure the encroaching darkness does not consume your progress or extinguish your creative flames.
Are there restrictions on the number of sorcerers per team?
Teams may consist of 2 to 4 members, allowing for balanced covens that can tackle challenges without overwhelming the arcane forces.
What if I arrive late to the midnight gathering?
Late registration is permitted until the stroke of midnight, but early arrival is advised to avoid the wrath of the event's dark schedule.
